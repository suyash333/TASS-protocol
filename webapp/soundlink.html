~TASS SoundLink
signed TASS records over sound — 16-tone MFSK acoustic modem, entirely in your browser
converter · protocol repo
1 · Sign — record is canonicalized and MAC-tagged (~!:) with your shared passphrase 2 · Transmit — bytes become 4-bit tones, 1800–4050 Hz, 64 ms/symbol ≈ 7.8 B/s 3 · Receive — another device listens, decodes, and verifies the MAC before trusting the record
▶ Transmit
◉ Receive idle
Use two devices (or two browser tabs on one machine with the volume up). Sound is a broadcast, spoofable medium — the ~! MAC is what lets the receiver trust what it heard. Demo uses HMAC-SHA-256 via WebCrypto (the Python library uses HMAC-SHA3-256; same construction). Full protocol details: book, chapter 11.
